  • Registration

  • Having a problem when trying to Log in to MedicineStudies?

    Did you know that you can register for a newsletter without being fully registered for the MedicineStudies site? One of the most common comments we receive, "I get your newsletters but I can't remember my username and password?" To fully register with MedicineStudies (go ahead, it's FREE), complete the online registration form at: https://member.MedicineStudies.com/register.aspx. Once you complete the registration process, you will receive an email asking you to confirm your email address. Follow the link in that email to confirm your email address. Once confirmed, your newsletter registration will merge with your MedicineStudies.com registration. You then have access to log in and join the interactive areas, like MedicineStudies Health Exchange. You will also have access to all of our tools, like the Target HEART Rate Calculator, the Food and Fitness Planner, and more.

  • I want to change my screen name.

    We get this question a lot - your screen name is tied to your email address. You do have a few options, included below: You can create a new account, choosing a different screen name, if you use a different email address. Or, if you wish to create a new account with your same email address, we can delete your current account (there is a 2 day time delay between deleting an account and the ability to create a new account). Once the deletion process has completed, you may create a new profile and new screen name for your email address. Please submit a ticket through "Contact Us" button if you wish to delete your current account.

  • You Receive MedicineStudies Newsletters But Can’t Log in

    MedicineStudies site registration is separate from MedicineStudies newsletter subscription. To fully register with MedicineStudies, complete the online registration form at: https://member.MedicineStudies.com/register.aspx. Once you complete the registration process, you will receive an email asking you to confirm your email address. After your email address is confirmed, your newsletter profile will merge with your MedicineStudies registration. You then will be able to log in and join the interactive areas, like MedicineStudies Health Exchange and fully use all of our tools.
